Destination overview

Paradise Island is the tropical paradise located in the heart of the Pacific Ocean. With its crystal-clear waters and lush landscapes, it's a dream destination for travellers seeking relaxation and adventure. Whether you want to unwind on sun-kissed beaches or embark on thrilling outdoor activities, Paradise Island has something for everyone.

Paradise Island is a scheduled oasis, far removed from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. The air is filled with the sweet scent of tropical flowers, and the gentle rustle of palm leaves provides a soothing soundtrack to your escape. As you step onto the shores, you are greeted by the warm embrace of the tropical sun and the rhythmic pull of ocean waves.

The coral reefs that surround Paradise Island are a kaleidoscope of colors, housing an abundance of marine life that beckons snorkelers and divers to explore their vibrant underwater world. From playful dolphins to graceful sea turtles, the marine inhabitants add to the allure of this aquatic paradise.

Activities

  • Relaxing on pristine white-sand beaches

  • Participating in water sports such as kayaking and paddleboarding

Travel Tips

Best Time to Visit

Year around but peak season is during the dry monthes of June to September.

Consider visiting during weekdays for fewer crowds

Currency

Local Currency is Paradise Dollars
Major Credit cards are widely accepted

Language

English and Paradesion Creole are widely spoken.
Learn a few local phrases to interact with locals.

Local Cuisine

Indulge with wonderful local Flavours with fusion of seafood and Aromatic Species.

Weather Conditions

While the weather is always Sunny, ocassional tropical showers can occur. It's advisable to carry an Umbrella
Check weatherforecast before planning outdoor activities.
